About Us

Skinfinery is a skin, hair, nail and cosmetic clinic in Ahmedabad established by Dr. Priyanjali Vaishnav. She is a qualified and board certified dermatologist. She also has special interest in trichology and cosmetology.

She did her MBBS from Geetanjali Medical College and Hospital, Udaipur( Rajasthan University of health sciences) & post graduation in Dermatology from Sir Sayajirao General (SSG) Hospital, Baroda( MS University).

She is also a fellow of IADVL( Indian association of Dermatology, Venereology and Leprosy). Here, we at Skinfinery clinic offer a quality and scientific evidence based treatments to our patients at affordable prices.
